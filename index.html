Google Keep Desktop for OSX

A Super Simple Desktop Client for Mac OSX Built in Javascript and MacGap

Why

Google Keep is an amazing tool for keeping track of your lists and notes. Unfortunately, you're limited to the in-browser experience unless you use Chrome. Google Keep Desktop for OSX simply wraps the Google Keep site in an app and allows you to quickly and easily access it as a separate entity outside of having to navigate through your browser tabs.
